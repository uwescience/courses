Keith Cieplicki

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Keith Cieplicki is a former college women's basketball coach. He coached at Vermont, where he became their all time winningest coach. He posted a 127-53 (.7055) record while at Vermont, where he coached from 1997 to 2003. He left Vermont to take the head coaching job at Syracuse. In his three seasons there, he posted a 28-55 overall record. His tenure was marked by players, coaches, and staff leaving the program. After the 2006 season he resigned.

While at Vermont, his teams won three American East Conference regular season titles, and advanced to the NCAA tournament in 2000. In 2000, and 2002, he was named American East Conference Coach of the year. He also served as an assistant coach at Boston College. He played college basketball at the College of William & Mary, where he was named a three time all-conference selection.
