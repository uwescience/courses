Koo Ki-Lan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Koo Ki-Lan (born March 10, 1977) is a female volleyball player who represented South Korea at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney, Australia. There she ended up in 8th place with the Women's National Team. Two years later, at the World Championship in Germany, she was named Best Digger and Best Receiver.

[edit] Honours

  • 2000 FIVB World Grand Prix — 5th place
  • 2000 Olympic Games — 8th place
  • 2001 World Grand Champions Cup — 6th place
  • 2002 World Championship — 6th place
  • 2003 FIVB World Cup — 9th place
