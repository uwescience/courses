Kitsuné Maison Compilation 1

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Kitsuné Maison Compilation 1 is the first compilation released by the French label Kitsuné Music. It was released on 3 October 2005.

  2. Exchpoptrue - Lost and Found
  3. Digitalism - Zdarlight
  4. Ruede Hagelstein - Eclectic People
  5. Tomboy - Samba!
  6. Popular Computer - Clone Age
  7. Architecture In Helsinki - Do The Whirlwind
  8. Italian X-Rays - Strip Down
  9. VHS or Beta - Night On Fire (Carlos D Remix)
  10. Tom Vek - I Ain't Saying My Goodbyes
  11. Lo-Fi-Fnk - Change Channel
  12. Hot Chip - I Don't Know the Half of How the Hell to Keep from Holding Back
  13. Metronomy - This Could Be Beautiful
