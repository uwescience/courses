Blumenkrantz

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blumenkrantz is a German and Ashkenazi Jewish surname whose English translation is "Flower-wreath". It may refer to:

  • Avrohom Blumenkrantz, American rabbi
  • Jeff Blumenkrantz, American composer
This page or section lists people with the surname Blumenkrantz. If an internal link for a specific person referred you to this page, you may wish to add the given name(s) to that link.
