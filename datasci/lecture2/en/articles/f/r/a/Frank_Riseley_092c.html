Frank Riseley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Frank Lorymer Riseley (born on 6 July 1877 in Clifton, Bristol – 6 February 1959 in Torquay) was a British male tennis player. He won the Wimbledon Double Championships twice in 1902 and 1906. He lost the singles finals three times against Lawrence Doherty in 1903, 1904 and 1905.
