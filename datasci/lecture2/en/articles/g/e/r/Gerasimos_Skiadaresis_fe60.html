Born 1960
Patras, Greece
Occupation actor

Gerasimos Skiadaresis (Greek: Γεράσιμος Σκιαδαρέσης, b. 1960 in Patras) is a Greek actor. He also worked in theatre in E. Chatzikou School which he began in 1982. He is married to an actress Bessy Malfa.

[edit] Theatre

  • La Moschetta
  • A play from Moliére
  • Louves
  • Oleanna (David Mamet)
  • I stella me ta kokkina gantia (Η Στέλλα με τα κόκκινα γάντια = Stella With Red Gloves, by Iakavos Kabanellis)
  • After The Rain
