Wara Seoni is a city and a municipality in Balaghat district in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India census[1], Wara Seoni had a population of 22,966. Males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. Wara Seoni has an average literacy rate of 76%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 82%, and female literacy is 70%. In Wara Seoni, 12% of the population is under 6 years of age.
