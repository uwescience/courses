Juventud Aprista Peruana

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Juventud Aprista Peruana is the youth organization of Partido Aprista Peruano in Peru. It is a member of the International Union of Socialist Youth. JAP used to be a member of the World Federation of Democratic Youth.
