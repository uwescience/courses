Ali Suliman

Born Nazareth, Israel
Years active 2003 - Present

Ali Suliman (Arabic: علي سليمان‎, Hebrew: עלי סולימאן‎) (born in 1977) is a Muslim Israeli Arab actor from Nazareth of Palestinian descent. He starred in Paradise Now, which won the Golden Globe award for Best Foreign Film. In 2007, he appeared in the movie The Kingdom, opposite Jamie Foxx, Jennifer Garner, and Chris Cooper.
