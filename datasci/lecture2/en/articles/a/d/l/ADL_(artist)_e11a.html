ADL (artist)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

ADL is a Swedish-American rapper based in Stockholm, Sweden. He has enjoyed success as a solo artist but has also collaborated with many other artists in the Stockholm music scene, among them are Blacknuss All Stars and Awa Manneh.

In the 1990s ADL was very outspoken against the growing wave of racism in Sweden and participated in a number of debates about the trend. He was also part on a forum discussing the growing reluctation against multiculturalism in Sweden in particular and Europe in general, which aired on MTV Europe.

In the later years he has collaborated with the Danish Nobody Beats The Beats music collective on many tracks, including the popular single "Straight From The Heart".
