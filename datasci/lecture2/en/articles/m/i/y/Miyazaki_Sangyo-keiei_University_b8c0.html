Miyazaki Sangyo-keiei University

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Miyazaki Sangyo-keiei University (宮崎産業経営大学, Kagoshima sangyō keiei daigaku?) is a private university in Miyazaki, Miyazaki, Japan. The predecessor of the school was founded in 1923, and it was chartered as a university in 1987.
