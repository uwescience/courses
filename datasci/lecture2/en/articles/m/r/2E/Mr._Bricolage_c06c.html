Mr. Bricolage is a French hypermarket chain offering home improvement and do-it-yourself goods. The chain operates over 500 stores in these countries:

  • Romania
  • Andorra
  • Belgium
  • Bulgaria
  • Spain
  • Argentina
  • Uruguay
  • Morocco
  • Madagascar
