Mladen Lazarević

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mladen Lazarević (Serbian Cyrillic: Младен Лазаревић; born January 16, 1984) is a Serbian football player currently playing for FK Partizan as a defender.

Lazarević joined Partizan from newly relegated FK Zemun.
