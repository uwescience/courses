Dering Baronets

There has been one creation of baronets with the surname Dering. It became extinct on the death of the 12th Baronet Rupert Anthony Yea Dering who died 16 Mar 1975.

[edit] Dering of Surrenden Dering, Kent (1 February 1626)

  • Sir Edward Dering, 5th Baronet (c.1706 - 15 Apr 1762)

  • Sir Edward Dering, 7th Baronet (16 Feb 1757 - 30 Jun 1811)
