The Cat (1992 film)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Cat (老貓) is a 1992 Hong Kong film directed by Lam Ngai Kai.

[edit] Cast and roles

  • Philip Kwok - Wang Chieh-Mei
  • Lau Siu-Ming - Errol
  • Waise Lee - Wei Si Li (Wisely)
  • Christine Ng - Pai So
  • Gloria Yip - Alien Girl
