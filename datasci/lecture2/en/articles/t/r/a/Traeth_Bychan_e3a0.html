Traeth Bychan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Traeth Bychan (Bychan Beach in English) is a small but popular beach on the isle of Anglesey, or Môn. There are no facilities there, so, if you go there, you will need to take a picnic with you. Nearby, is the small town of Benllech and the village of Marian-Glas.
