Tom Daly

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tom Daly can refer to:

  • Tom Daly (politician) (born 1955) — Orange County Clerk-Recorder and former Mayor of Anaheim, California
  • Tom Daly (baseball catcher) (1891–1946) — former Major League Baseball catcher for the Chicago White Sox (1913–1915), Cleveland Indians (1916), and Chicago Cubs (1918–1921), as well as a Boston Red Sox coach (1933–1946)
  • Tom Daly (baseball infielder) (1866–1938) — former Major League Baseball second baseman for the Chicago Cubs (1887–1888), Washington Nationals (1889), Brooklyn Dodgers (1890–1901), Chicago White Sox (1902–1903), and Cincinnati Reds (1903)
  • Tom Daly (film producer) — film producer with the National Film Board of Canada[1]
