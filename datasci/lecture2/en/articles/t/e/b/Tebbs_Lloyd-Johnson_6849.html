Terrence 'Tebbs' Lloyd-Johnson (Apr 7, 1900 - Dec 26, 1984) was a British speed-walker.

Lloyd-Johnson won the bronze medal in the 50 kilometre walk at the 1948 Summer Olympics in London at the age of 48 and 15 days, thereby becoming the oldest Olympic medal winning athlete.
