Lockdown 2005 was the first annual Lockdown professional wrestling pay-per-view produced by Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA). It took place on April 24, 2005 from the iMPACT! Zone in Orlando, Florida. As it was a Lockdown event, all of the matches were held inside of "Six Sides of Steel".[1]

[edit] Results

Numbers in parentheses indicate the length of the match.

  • Dark match: 3Live Kru (Ron Killings and Konnan) defeated David Young and Lex Lovett, and The Naturals (Andy Douglas and Chase Stevens) (w/Chris Candido) in a Triple Threat match (6:34)
    • Killings pinned Lovett after a Sitout Double Underhook Facebuster.
  • Apolo and Sonny Siaki defeated Chris Candido and Lance Hoyt in a Six Sides of Steel match (6:53)
    • Siaki pinned Hoyt after a Top Rope Splash.
    • After the match The Naturals came out and attacked Hoyt, delivering the Natural Disaster.
    • Candido died four days later after having surgery for an injury suffered during this match.
  • Dustin Rhodes defeated Bobby Roode (w/Coach D'Amore and A-1) in a Two out of Three Falls Six Sides of Steel match (15:15)
    • Roode pinned Rhodes with a Small Package (0:50)
    • Rhodes pinned Roode after a Diving Bulldog (11:00)
    • Rhodes pinned Roode after hitting him with a steel chair. (15:15)
      • Both men were blindfolded for the third fall.
  • Shocker defeated Michael Shane (w/Trinity), Sonjay Dutt and Chris Sabin (w/Traci) in an Xscape match (15:35)
    • Shocker pinned Dutt after a Corkscrew Elbowdrop (10:56)
    • Sabin pinned Shane after a Cradle Shock (14:03)
    • Shocker won by escaping the cage to become #1 Contender to the TNA X Division Championship (15:35)
  • Jeff Hardy defeated Raven in a Six Sides of Steel Table match (14:01)
    • Hardy won after a Leg Drop from the top of the cage through four stacked-up tables.
  • America's Most Wanted (Chris Harris and James Storm) defeated Team Canada (Petey Williams and Eric Young) (w/A-1) in a Six Sides of Steel Strap match to retain the NWA World Tag Team Championship (15:10)
    • Storm pinned Williams after a Death Sentence.
  • Christopher Daniels defeated Elix Skipper in a Six Sides of Steel match to retain the TNA X Division Championship (15:23)
    • Daniels pinned Skipper after the Angel's Wings.
  • Sean Waltman, Diamond Dallas Page and B.G. James defeated Jeff Jarrett, Monty Brown and The Outlaw in a Lethal Lockdown match (15:38)
    • Waltman pinned Brown with a Small Package.
    • Kevin Nash was originally scheleded to be in the match, but was replaced by B.G. James due to an injury.
  • A.J. Styles defeated Abyss in a Six Sides of Steel match (19:58)
    • Styles pinned Abyss after a Sunset Flip Powerbomb off the cage into a pile of tacks to become #1 contender to the NWA World Heavyweight Championship.
