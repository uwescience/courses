Vgo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the gaming podcast, see Video Game Outsiders.
Vgo signature with sickle-shaped G inside Apt Crypt

Vgo, or Ugo, for "Hughes", was a stone mason active in Provence during the 12th century. He left his signature on several Romanesque religious edifices in Provence and mainly in Tricastin:

  • Vaison Cathedral
  • Notre Dame d'Aubune
  • St Blaise de Bauzon
  • St Sepulchre Chapel in Beaumont
  • Crypt of Apt Cathedral
