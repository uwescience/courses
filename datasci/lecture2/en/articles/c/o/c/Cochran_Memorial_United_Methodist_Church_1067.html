Cochran Memorial United Methodist Church

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Philip G. Cochran Memorial United Methodist Church is a cathedral-style church building located in Dawson, Pennsylvania, USA. A United Methodist facility and congregation, it has been on the National Register of Historic Places since 1982.
