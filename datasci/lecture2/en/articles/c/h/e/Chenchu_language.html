Chenchu language

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Chenchu is a South Indian language from Andhra Pradesh which is spoken by about 28,754 (1981 census) persons. It is also called as Chenchucoolam, Chenchwar, Chenswar or Choncharu.

It is classifeid as Dravidian, South-Central, Telugu.

Code: ISO/DIS 639-3
